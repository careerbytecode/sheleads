✦ Before You Start

Mandatory Program Rules

Every participant must follow ALL rules without exception. These ensure maximum learning and a consistent professional experience.

💻
DeviceLaptop or Desktop ONLY. Mobile phones are not suitable for class work.
🌐
BrowserGoogle Chrome ONLY for all class work and assignments.
🔒
Chrome UsageNo WhatsApp Web, personal Gmail, or social media inside your CareerByteCode Chrome profile.
📡
InternetStable broadband or Wi-Fi only. No mobile hotspot during class.
🔑
Digital IdentityOne Gmail · One Username · Same handle everywhere. Consistency is non-negotiable.
🗄️
CredentialsStore your username, password, and recovery email safely at all times.
🖥️
Screen SharingKnow how to share full screen and a Chrome-only window before every class.
🌍
Africa ExceptionDev.to alone is acceptable if stable broadband is unavailable in your region.
